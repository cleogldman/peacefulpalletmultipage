Art Journaling Start an art journal where you combine writing with drawing and collage making. Use it as a personal space to document your emotions, experiences, and thoughts in a creative way
Collage Making Collect old magazines, newspapers, colored paper, and glue. Cut out images, words, or phrases that resonate with you or express your emotions. Arrange and glue these cutouts onto a larger sheet of paper to create a collage that represents your feelings or aspirations.
Free Drawing and Expression Gather your art supplies and start drawing freely without any specific goal in mind. Allow your emotions to guide your hand and express yourself through line, color, and shapes.
Mindful Coloring Get an adult coloring book or print out intricate designs Color mindfully, focusing on each stroke and the sensations it brings, concentrating on the act of coloring can be relaxing and meditative
